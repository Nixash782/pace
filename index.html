Create an Account
Sign up for a free account on our website or app.
Complete your profile with your fitness goals, preferences, and any medical conditions.
Browse available trainers and services to find the right fit for you.

To find a trainer,
go to the services section on the website,under the training programs.
You can browse through profiles, filter by specialization
(e.g., weight loss, strength training, yoga), availability, and location.
Each profile includes the trainer’s bio, certifications, ratings,
and user reviews to help you choose the right fit for your goals.

Booking a session is simple:
Visit your chosen trainer’s profile.
Click “Book Session”.
Select your preferred date and time from the trainer’s available slots.
Confirm your booking and complete payment (if required).
You’ll receive a confirmation email or
notification once the session is booked

To cancel a session:
Log in to your account and go to “My Bookings”.
Find the session you want to cancel and click “Cancel”.
Confirm the cancellation.
Please note that cancellations must be made at least [X hours]
in advance to avoid a cancellation fee. Check our cancellation
policy for full details.
